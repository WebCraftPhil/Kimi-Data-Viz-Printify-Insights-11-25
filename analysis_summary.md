# Printify Sales Analysis Summary

## Executive Summary
This comprehensive analysis of your Printify sales data reveals key insights into your print-on-demand business performance over 43 weeks in 2025. The analysis shows positive growth trends with seasonal variations and identifies strategic opportunities for business optimization.

## Key Performance Metrics

### Financial Performance
- **Total Revenue**: $6,213.33
- **Average Weekly Revenue**: $144.50
- **Peak Revenue Week**: $298.20 (April 21, 2025)
- **Total Items Sold**: 241 units
- **Average Items per Week**: 5.6 units

### Growth Trends
- **Revenue Growth**: Steady upward trajectory with seasonal peaks
- **Seasonal Pattern**: Strongest performance in Q2 (March-May)
- **Consistency**: 43 weeks of continuous operation
- **Moving Average**: 4-week average shows stable growth pattern

## Data Analysis Framework

### 1. Temporal Analysis
- **Time Series**: Weekly sales data from January 6 to October 27, 2025

### 2. Product Performance
